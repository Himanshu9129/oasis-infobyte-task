ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE

AI, which stands for "Artificial Intelligence," refers to the development of computer systems and software that can perform tasks typically requiring human intelligence. These tasks include learning, problem-solving, reasoning, decision-making, understanding natural language, and recognizing patterns.

MANAGEMENT

Management is the process of planning, organizing, leading, and controlling resources, including people, finances, materials, and information, to achieve specific organizational goals and objectives effectively and efficiently. It is a fundamental function within organizations, whether they are businesses, government agencies, non-profit organizations, or other entities.

CYBER SECURITY

Cybersecurity refers to the practice of protecting computer systems, networks, data, and digital assets from unauthorized access, attacks, damage, or theft. CLOUD COMPUTING

Cloud computing is a technology paradigm that involves delivering a wide range of computing services, including servers, storage, databases, networking, software, analytics, and more, over the internet.

PROGRAMMING LANGUAGE

A programming language is a formal system of communication used to instruct a computer or a computing device to perform specific tasks or operations. It serves as a bridge between human-readable instructions and the machine code that computers understand.

SALES AND MARKETING

Sales is the process of directly engaging with potential customers or clients to persuade them to purchase a product or service and Marketing encompasses a broader range of activities focused on creating awareness, generating interest, and attracting potential customers to a product or service.
